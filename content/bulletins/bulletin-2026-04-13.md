### 🎫 Join or Renew ARRL via the Club Website
Help the club secure commissions ($15 for first-time signups, $5 for renewals) by routing your American Radio Relay League entries directly through our executive desk. 
* **The Process:** Download the [ARRL Membership Application CLUB PDF](https://www.arrl.org/files/file/Membership/Club%20Commission/ARRL%20Membership%20Application%20CLUB%20rev%20Jan%202024.pdf).
* Print, fill out, and mail or hand the application block to our Club Treasurer along with a check made out directly to ARRL.
  👉 *Treasurer Mail Stop:* **Ephraim Botnick (WQ4N), 547 Riverwood Drive, Bolivia, NC 28422**
