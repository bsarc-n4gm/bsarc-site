🎉 Happy New Year from the Board of Directors!

Welcome to 2026! The board wishes a fantastic new year of operating, contesting, and technical experimentation to all Brunswick Shores Amateur Radio Club members, families, and associates. Let’s make this an outstanding year for local amateur radio infrastructure and public emergency readiness!

🏕️ Operations Check: Winter Field Day Countdown

Get your cold-weather gear sorted out, your portable power battery arrays tested, and your portable wire antennas prepped! Winter Field Day 2026 is rapidly closing in.

  • When: Saturday & Sunday, January 24-25, 2026
  • Venue Checkpoint: Dutchman’s Creek County Park (4750 Fish Factory Rd SE, Southport, NC 28461)
  • The Mission: Exercising emergency communications deployment logistics in harsh weather scenarios. If you want to assist with setup crews or join the logging roster matrices, connect with the field operations committee.

📡 Weekly Local Radio Net Alignments

Our recurring local emergency communication tracking loops remain fully configured for your local signal and traffic check-ins:

  • 🎙️ The Shrimp Net: Every Monday morning at 8:30 AM via the 147.315 MHz machine (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
  • 🛡️ Brunswick County ARES Net: Every Monday evening at 7:00 PM using the same 147.315 MHz repeater layout (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
