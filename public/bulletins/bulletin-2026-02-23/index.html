🎉 Next Weekend: North Carolina QSO Party Activation

Get your shacks configured, your wire tracking dipoles tuned, and your logging laptops ready to capture coordinates! The annual North Carolina QSO Party officially fires up next weekend.

  • When: Sunday, March 1, 2026 from 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM EST
  • Mode: On the Air (HF SSB/CW bands)
  • Mission: Let’s put Brunswick County on the airwaves map! Work as many in-state and out-of-state stations as possible. Bonus station coordinates and logging submission rules are updated on the state logging gateway channels.
