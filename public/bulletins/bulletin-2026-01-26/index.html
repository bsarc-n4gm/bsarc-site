🤝 Winter Field Day: Outstanding Support and Participation

An exceptional round of appreciation and thank you to every operator, technical engineer, setup helper, and visitor who supported the club deployment at Dutchman’s Creek County Park this past weekend! Despite cold temperatures, our community turnout was brilliant. Signal tracking logs confirm that multiple voice and digital HF links were successfully maintained under challenging conditions. Well done to everyone!

🧳 Regional Hamfests & Conventions Preview

  • 📆 Orlando HamCation 2026: Mark your calendar tracking parameters for February 13-14, 2026 in Orlando, FL! Hosted at the Central Florida Fairgrounds. 📡 Weekly Local Radio Net Alignments

Our recurring local emergency communication tracking loops remain fully configured for your local signal and traffic check-ins:

  • 🎙️ The Shrimp Net: Every Monday morning at 8:30 AM via the 147.315 MHz machine.
  • 🛡️ Brunswick County ARES Net: Every Monday evening at 7:00 PM using the same 147.315 MHz repeater layout (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
