💼 Operational Notice: Club Treasurer Term Transition

A volunteer is needed to step forward for the upcoming Club Treasurer position, as Ephraim Botnick (WQ4N) is planning to step away from this role at the conclusion of his current term.

Serving as a club officer is an excellent way to give back to your community. Full operational training and a clean inventory turnover will be provided to ensure a smooth administrative transition.

⚠️ Operations Compliance Reminder: Per our club’s constitutional bylaws, the Treasurer stands as one of the three (3) mandatory core officer positions strictly required by law to maintain continuous club operations. Reach out to President Mark Sergent (KA4EVR) or any board member for more information.

📡 Weekly Local Radio Net Alignments

Our recurring localized radio networks remain fully configured for your operational testing:

  • 🎙️ The Shrimp Net: Every Monday morning at 8:30 AM via the 147.315 MHz machine (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
  • 🛡️ Brunswick County ARES Net: Every Monday evening at 7:00 PM using the same 147.315 MHz repeater (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
  • 🏠 AUXCOM / ARES Operations Meeting: Bi-monthly at 5:00 PM inside the BEMC Building in Supply, NC. This occurs on months when the main business meeting is slated for the Supply facility.
