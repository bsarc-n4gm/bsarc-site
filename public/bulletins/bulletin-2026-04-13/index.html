🏎️ Public Service: Cape Fear Cruisers Car Club Show

BSARC will be providing tactical radio tracking and coordinate safety communications support for the upcoming Cape Fear Cruisers Car Club Show in Southport, NC.

  • Activation Date: Saturday, April 25, 2026
  • Volunteers Needed: Please coordinate your mobile rigs and handheld check-ins with the emergency operations committee. Public events like this are highly valued opportunities to showcase amateur radio utility to county services!

🎫 Join or Renew ARRL via the Club Website

Help the club secure commissions ($15 for first-time signups, $5 for renewals) by routing your American Radio Relay League entries directly through our executive desk.

  • The Process: Download the ARRL Membership Application CLUB PDF .
  • Print, fill out, and mail or hand the application block to our Club Treasurer along with a check made out directly to ARRL. 👉 Treasurer Mail Stop: Ephraim Botnick (WQ4N), 547 Riverwood Drive, Bolivia, NC 28422

📡 Weekly Local Radio Net Alignments

Our recurring localized radio networks remain fully configured for your operational testing:

  • 🎙️ The Shrimp Net: Every Monday morning at 8:30 AM via the 147.315 MHz machine (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
  • 🛡️ Brunswick County ARES Net: Every Monday evening at 7:00 PM using the same 147.315 MHz repeater (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
