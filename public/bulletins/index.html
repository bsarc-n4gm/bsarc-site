Weekly Bulletin: New HF SSB Net, ARES Rotations & Welcome Julia KR4LQQ

📡 Weekly Local Radio Net Alignments

Our persistent radio networks remain fully configured for your local check-ins:

  • 🎙️ The Shrimp Net: Every Monday morning at 8:30 AM via the 147.315 MHz machine (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
  • 🛡️ Brunswick County ARES Net: Every Monday evening at 7:00 PM using the same 147.315 MHz repeater (+600 offset, Tone 118.8 Hz).
  • 🏠 AUXCOM / ARES Operations Meeting: Bi-monthly at 5:00 PM at the BEMC Headquarters Building in Supply, NC. This meeting proceeds directly ahead of the business meeting on months when our main club gather shifts to the Supply facility.

💼 Urgent Notice: Club Treasurer Position Volunteer Needed

A volunteer is needed to step into the vital role of Club Treasurer, as Ephraim Botnick (WQ4N) will be stepping away at the completion of his current elected term.

This is an exceptional and rewarding opportunity to serve your fellow amateurs. A comprehensive training and hands-on operational turnover will be provided to ensure an absolute smooth transition for the newcomer.

⚠️ Organizational Operations Notice: Per our foundational corporate documents, the Treasurer is one of the three (3) mandatory core executive positions strictly required to sustain continual legal operations. If you have a desire to help look after the club’s financial logging, please contact President Mark Sergent (KA4EVR) or any board member.
