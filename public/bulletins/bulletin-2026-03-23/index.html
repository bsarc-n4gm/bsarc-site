🎙️ Experimental HF SSB Net Log: March Session Recap

The inaugural monthly experimental HF net was successfully held at 8:00 PM on Wednesday, March 18, 2026, on 80 meters. A big thank you to Gary Quinn (NC4S) for acting as Net Control!

The following stations successfully checked in to test propagation profiles across southeastern North Carolina and beyond: K4IR, WQ4N, K1YC, KI4W, and K4JCS. We look forward to next month’s session on April 15!
