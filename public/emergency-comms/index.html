Brunswick County Emergency Communications Portals

The Brunswick Shores Amateur Radio Club (BSARC) stands ready to provide robust, auxiliary wireless link channels during complete commercial network blackouts, severe winter storms, or coastal tropical storm landfalls.

To serve our community effectively, local public safety radio operations are split into two independent emergency programs. Select the appropriate portal below to view program directives, coordinator contacts, training task books, and application forms:

Amateur Radio Emergency Service (ARES®)

Organized under the ARRL national hobbyist program framework. Focused on auxiliary backup networks for community support groups and non-governmental aid organizations.

County EC: George Fryer (KI4KK)
Enter ARES Portal →

Auxiliary Communications (AUXCOMM)

Sanctioned by CISA / FEMA under DHS. Formally recognized and integrated directly into the Brunswick County Emergency Management agency framework for state and local infrastructure duties.

County Coordinator: Bill Sirginson (N4PEN)
