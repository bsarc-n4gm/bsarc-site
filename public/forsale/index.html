Welcome to the Brunswick Shores Amateur Radio Club gear hub. This combined marketplace serves a dual purpose: it hosts the active Member Swapfest for regional operators, and manages automated listings for official Club Surplus Equipment cleared for liquidation.

  • Club Loaner Assets: Full equipment database records can be reviewed via the tracking matrix link. Please note that equipment checkout privileges are strictly limited to active, paid club members only.
  • Club Surplus Sales: Transactions for listed club surplus items are handled directly by our Equipment Manager. Proceeds directly benefit club repeater funds.
  • Member Trade Listings: Provided as a courtesy to the local ham community. BSARC handles no financial transactions for individual listings—please communicate directly with the listed seller or buyer.
