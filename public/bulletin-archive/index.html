Welcome to the Brunswick Shores Amateur Radio Club official bulletin repository. Weekly updates are compiled by the club secretary to summarize localized emergency traffic nets, public safety coordination, upcoming VE testing configurations, and general business announcements.

The absolute latest release is pinned live on the main dashboard sidebar desk layout for immediate tracking.
